Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 2026

Bluewhale Properties (the "App") is a rental expense tracking tool built for small landlords. This policy confirms that the App operates with a Zero-Data Collection philosophy.

1. Data Collection

We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal information. This includes:

  • No Personal Identity: We do not access your name, email, or phone number.
  • No Accounts: No sign-in is required; all features are available immediately.
  • No Tracking: We do not use cookies, ad-trackers, or behavioral analytics.

2. Local Storage & Permissions

The App functions as a local utility. Your data stays under your control:

  • Google Drive Integration: The App can access only your personal Google Drive to store and retrieve your rental expense data. We do not store, access, or monitor any data on our servers—all data remains in your Google Drive.
  • Your Authorization: You control what the App can access and can revoke permissions at any time through your Google account settings.
  • No Server Storage: The App does not require an internet connection after initial setup and does not "call home" to external servers to transmit your financial data.

3. Children's Privacy

Because the App collects absolutely no data, it is fully compliant with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and is safe for athletes and students of all ages.

4. Third-Party Services

This App does not contain third-party advertisements, social media plug-ins, or links to external data brokers.
