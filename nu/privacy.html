Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 2026

nü (the "App") is a personal food journal and nutrition tracker. All data you enter stays on your device. This policy explains what permissions the App uses and why.

1. Data Collection

We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on external servers. This includes:

  • No Accounts: No sign-in is required; no username, email, or password is collected.
  • No Tracking: We do not use cookies, ad-trackers, or behavioral analytics.
  • No Third-Party Analytics: The App does not include any analytics SDK or crash-reporting service that transmits personal data.

2. Local Storage

All meal logs, nutrition data, and goals you create are stored locally on your device as JSON files in the App's private internal storage. This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly export or back it up yourself.

3. Permissions

  • Camera: Used exclusively to scan barcodes on food packaging. The camera feed is processed on-device to read the barcode number only; no image is captured, stored, or transmitted.
  • Internet: Used to query the Open Food Facts public API to look up nutrition information by barcode or food name. Only the barcode number or search term is sent — no personal data is included in these requests.
  • Health Connect (Read Weight, Read Active Calories Burned, Read Total Calories Burned, Read Exercise): Used to display your activity and body weight data alongside your nutrition log. The App reads this data locally from Health Connect and never transmits it to any server. You can revoke these permissions at any time through the Health Connect app.

4. Third-Party Services

The App queries the Open Food Facts API, a public, non-commercial food database. Only food search terms or barcode numbers are sent in these requests. Open Food Facts' own privacy policy applies to these requests.

The App reads data from Google Health Connect, subject to Google's Health Connect privacy policy. No health data is shared with any party other than what is already stored in Health Connect on your device.

5. Children's Privacy

Because the App does not collect any personal data, it is compliant with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and is safe for users of all ages.
